## Troubleshooting

### Signature doesn't appear
- Make sure you saved settings in Gmail
- Try composing a new email (not reply)
- Check that signature is set for "new emails"

### Links don't work
- Gmail may block links until email is sent
- Test by sending yourself an email
- Links work for recipients

### Image doesn't show
- Image is embedded as base64 (no external hosting needed)
- Should work in all email clients
- If image is too large, Gmail may compress it

### Colors look different
- Some email clients adjust colors
- Signature optimized for Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail
- Core information remains visible
